About

A biopsychosocial framework designed for rigorous clinical governance and sustainable outcomes.

Clinical philosophy

Positive Path Wellness is being developed around an integrated biopsychosocial framework: treat biology, psychology, and environment as interacting drivers of function and recovery.

What “integrative” means here

  • Clinical governance: protocols, supervision, documentation standards, and outcome-oriented care planning.
  • Evidence-based modalities: interventions selected for fit and efficacy, not trends.
  • Continuity: coordination across providers as appropriate.

Regulatory posture

We are planning to operate under NYS OMH Article 31 standards. Public-facing information will remain conservative until approvals and operating details are finalized.
