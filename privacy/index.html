Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-01-15

This policy explains how Positive Path Wellness collects and uses information from this website.

What we collect

  • Contact details you submit (e.g., name, email) via our interest forms.
  • Basic technical data (e.g., IP address, device/browser type) collected by our hosting/analytics providers for security and performance.

How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries and send opening updates you requested.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the website.

What we don’t do

  • This website is not intended for clinical intake. Do not submit medical or protected health information (PHI) through website forms.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing

We may share information with vendors who help us operate the site (e.g., hosting, forms/email delivery) and only for that purpose, subject to their terms.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from update emails at any time (if applicable).
  • Request deletion of your submitted information by contacting us.
